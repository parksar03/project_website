Who is Morgan Mercer?

Morgan Mercer is an innovator, a survivor, a CEO, and a founder. She is from North Carolina and received her education from East Carolina University where she studied business and marketing. Mercer is considered underrepresented due to her gender identity and her racial background.

Her Achievements

Mercer created and developed a virtual reality program called Vantage Point, which focuses on sexual assault education. This education is done through real-life simulation of workplace harassment training and inclusion training. This program has and will continue to be instrumental in making our world more understanding and better educated about sexual assault and its importance.
